Campus life

School Church

Was estabilished in 1978
\

This church was built on the school campus to allow the students and teachers to offer their prayers before starting the day. Everyone loved the ambience of the church and felt peaceful and safe inside the church building. Students were also encouraged to bring their family members to this church if they wanted to pray together. But this was only allowed on Sundays and not during the week when school was in session.



School Library

Was estabilished in 1978
\

The school library was a place frequently viseted by students to studey or read storys. Many students would come using their library card to check out book to use to study on for a project or for an important exam. The library was also used as an quiet place for student to sudy in 30 minuts before and 1 hour after school, and durring break or free time.



School auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school auditorium was used for scholl events,shows, and preformences.The auditorium is also very big to fit at the least the number of students at the school with a maximum capacity of 3,500 people, also the auditorium has an humoungus stage for every type of preformance with a gargantuan projector screen. The school also lets people rent out the auditourium for public uses like events on days when shool is not in session.
